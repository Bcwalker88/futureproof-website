TV antenna installation FAQs

How much does antenna installation cost?

Installed antenna packages typically start at $299, with premium Australian-made options from $375. Final pricing depends on hardware choice, roof access and cabling requirements.

Do I need a new antenna or just a repair?

We run a signal and cabling test first. If your current antenna can be restored with a mast, connector or cable repair, we recommend repair before full replacement.

How long does antenna installation take?

Most standard installs and repairs are completed in around 1 to 2 hours. Multi-outlet balancing, difficult roof access, or long cable runs can take longer.
